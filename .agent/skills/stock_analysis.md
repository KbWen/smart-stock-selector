# Stock Analysis Skill

This skill provides specialized knowledge for the Taiwan Stock market analysis.

## Key Capabilities

- **Technical Analysis**: Deep understanding of RSI, MACD, KD, for the TWSE context.
- **Sniper Strategy**: Expertise in 3:1 Risk/Reward quantitative modeling.
- **Data Pipeline**: Knowledge of `yfinance` and `twstock` integration for historical and real-time data.

## Usage Guidelines

- When asked to analyze a stock, prioritize the **Rise Score V2** components.
- Always check for **Bollinger Squeeze** as a lead indicator for explosive moves.
- Refer to `core/analysis.py` for specific implementation of KD and ATR.
